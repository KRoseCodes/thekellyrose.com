About Me

Curriculum is one of my greatest passions. I love learning and creating curriculum allows me to share this new knowledge with all kinds of people! Over the last three years I have developed new technology curriculum that goes beyond the set standards to truly engage people in the learning process. This expereince has allowed me to develop my skills and hone my abilities to reach people of all ages through a variety of platforms from in person instruction to technoloigcal platforms and educational videos that I have started to develop. I always have a new avenue to explore with technology: always something new to learn and share. I always have a project outside of work that keeps my skills on the cutting edge - from developing additional curriculum for areas I am not responsible for to teaching myself new coding languages to better understand the infromation I am departing, I enjoy a good challange.

I've been enamored with understanding how different fields are connected for as long as I can remember. I've pursued my interests and always found ways to connect them back to each other. This ability to make cross-curricular connections as proven to be an outstanding advantage. Not only can I see the big picture, but I have enough experience in a wide variety of fields to be able to bring that vision to frutition. What I don't know I learn. It's this passion for learning, design, technology, and people, that inspires me everyday.

My career in education has been (and still is!) one of the most rewarding times of my life. I felt called to educate students as I wish I had been: by someone passionate about their subject matter and who would encourage intellectual pursuits. Today I want to bring that passion for learning back to adults. Wether someone is conducting independent resaerch or is required to do e-learning through their company, I want anyone who learns from me to walk away with a greater understanding of the topic and confidence in their ability to apply that knowledge right away.
